Welcome!

Oriel Science is a new Science Outreach and Public Engagement Centre, opening soon. It will host rolling exhibitions based on Swansea University’s Science research and on wider scientific themes of relevance to our daily lives. The Gallery (Oriel is Welsh for Gallery) will take snapshots of University research, craft them into compelling exhibits, place them in a dramatic setting designed to engage, educate and enthuse all. Oriel Science will be the shop-front for Swansea University's Science research and will be a vehicle for public engagement delivering wide ranging outreach programmes to schools and the public.
